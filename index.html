On Awakening...

Sunday on awakening...

Because I didn't get all my phone stuff done before I sent out last night's list, I'm including it here...

Decided to root so unlocked boot loader, extracted OTA package of ROM, installed magisk and patched boot.img

Joined Sober AF right before signing the patched boot.img with custom keys.

After the meeting, finished signing the patched image and flashed the patched image with the rest for ROM OTA zip.  Then re-locked bootloader.

Didn't brick phone!  Now have a Pixel 10 Pro XL with rooted GrapheneOS.  ::happy face::

Completed initial setup and started transfer of backed up user data to new device.

Got ready and went to bed.

Woke up not wanting to get up after tossing and turning most the night.

Immediately asked God, "Please direct my thinking. Let my thoughts be divorced from self-pity, dishonest or self-seeking motives, so that I might have a more contemplative mind."(p.86)

"Show me the way of patience, tolerance, kindliness and love."(p.83)

"Let me be sensible, tactful, considerate and humble."(p.83)

"May I be kind, caring, compassionate, understanding and loving toward all today."(p.86)

Considered my plans for the day and tried to go back sleep until I got a call from my mother singing me happy birthday.

Talked to her briefly but she could tell I was still in bed and let me go.

It didn't take long to realize I probably wouldn't sleep more so...

Rolled out of bed to my knees...
    
"I offer myself to You, to build with me and to do with me as You will. Relieve me of the bondage of self, that I may better do Your will. Take away my difficulties, that victory over them may bear witness to those I would help of Your power, Your love and Your way of life. May I do Your will always!"(p.63)

"I am now willing that you should have all of me, good and bad. I pray that you now remove from me every single defect of character which stands in the way of my usefulness to you and my fellows. Grant me strength, as I go out from here, to do your bidding." (p.76)

Got up and read page 167 of "Daily reflections" and page 159 of "Courage to Change".

Said a few set prayers that I selected and memorized that emphasize the principals discussed in the big book.(p. 87)
(serenity prayer, knots prayer and peace prayer.)

"Grant me freedom from self will today. Show me what my next step is to be and grant me the ability to take care of it."(p.87)

"May your will be done for those sick and suffering. Please show me what I can do to help."(p.84)

"Help me be effective in bringing others to you so they can find peace and comfort in their own lives."(p.84)

"Thank you for the life I have today and the people in it."(p.84)

"Show me what can I can do for the man who is still sick?"(p.164)

"Thy will be done." (p.88)

“Faith without works is dead.”(p.88)(James 2:26)
    
Responded to messages and sent out morning list.

Going to watch movies and work on getting new phone setup to be used as daily driver.

Top of Texas for 7pm big book study meeting.

Sober AF at 22:30.

Have a great day! (Unless you have other plans)
